Structure of Legal Link Attorneys

is a firm of Advocates, Legal Consultants and business Consultants having its offices at Ufukoni Road/Sea View. Apt. No. 001/Plot No. 2346/4 Dar es Salaam. The firm, Legal Link ATTORNEYS, was established on the 11th day of August 2000, formerly known as MSK Law Partners (Advocates), therefore it has experience of business for more than fifteen years now and it is incorporated under Chapter 213, R.E. 2002, of the Laws of Tanzania. This firm is a Partnership.

Legal Link Attorneys

is constituted with Two Principal Partners and one Associate Partner Mr. Karoli Valerian Tarimo, who are well qualified and experienced. All partners

Legal Link Attorneys

are advocates of the High Court of Tanzania with vast experience and extensive expertise in various legal disciplines. Apart from the Partners, LLA has employed advocates and legal Officers. The firm has employed Ms. Victoria Gregory, Irene Peter, Kephas Mayenje, Peter Ereneus and Justine Magafu advocates and five senior legal officers.

Legal Link Attorneys

has also supporting staff which includes two secretaries, three clerks to the Chambers, one office attendant, three guards and two drivers.

SCOPE OF ACTIVITIES

being a firm of Advocates legal and business consultants conducts all activities normally done by firms of Advocates and/ or solicitors. However the firm's specialization ties more in Banking and Commercial law, Labor Law, Tax Law, Insurance law, Landlord and Tenant, Insolvency and Bankruptcies, Land law and Conveyance, Corporate law, Arbitration, International Investments, Environmental Law, law relating to Children and Women Rights, Maritime law, Business law, Contracts.
